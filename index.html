ESVA Housing Mission

Making Homes Warmer, Safer, Drier, and Healthier

ESVA Nonprofit

The ESVA Housing Mission is a new nonprofit on the Eastern Shore of Virginia that provides critical home repair for those in need. ESVA Housing is a Christian mission organization dedicated to leveraging volunteers to help their neighbors in need.

Are You Aware?

Statistics show that of the 14,152 households in Accomack County, 2,051 are at or below the poverty level. Of the 5,292 residents in Northampton, 853 are at or below poverty.

Projects

Typical projects take approximately six hours with 12-15 volunteers. No group is obligated to work on all the projects. They can take on whatever their schedule allows.

Volunteer

The demand for our services is rapidly increasing due to the growing aging population and the number in need. Want to help us?

  • Have an open heart, a willingness to help others, and compassion for your neighbors without judgment.
  • Pick a Volunteer Leader for your team who will organize the team and work directly with ESVA Housing Mission to coordinate the project.
  • Get a team together. Find a group of like-minded volunteers to form a team. Wheelchair ramps take approximately 15 volunteers, but there may be other projects for smaller teams.
  • You get to pick the date. Contact us with your preferred project date and a rain date. Projects are typically completed in a single day!
  • Let us know if anyone on your team has construction experience and is willing to lead the project. If not, we will assign a Project Manager to you. No construction experience required!

Donate

Through volunteerism and donations, we successfully help families in Accomack and Northampton counties on the Eastern Shore of Virginia.

  1. Send a check to ESVA Housing Mission PO Box 277, Onancock VA 23417
  2. Use a Qualified Charitable Distribution (QCD) to gift some or all of your IRA minimum distribution (RMD), which saves you money if you do not have enough deductions to itemize. For some people, it helps reduce tax on Social Security.

3. Donate stocks or investments that have grown in value, eliminating capital gains taxes AND giving you a possible tax deduction. Have your custodian send the securities to: Charles Schwab; Acct. #6647-2122; DTC 0164; ESVA Housing Mission- John Fiege, Treasurer, 757-710-2489.

4. Retirement Plan Assets such as Traditional IRAs, 401(k), and 403(b) assets left to ESVA Housing Mission will transfer tax-free since we are a charity. 

5. Name ESVA Housing Mission as a beneficiary of your life insurance.
